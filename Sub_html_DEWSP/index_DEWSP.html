Enhancing the equal-weighted strategy using deep learning


Towards a better future with AI.
We develop an AI system that predicts stock markets based on deep learning algorithms. We focus on the development of a deep ensemble learning model that effectively combines several deep learning models into one predictive model, including feedforward, long short-term memory, convolutional neural networks, deep autoregressive networks and so on. It will have better generalization performance.
Make it yours with diversification.
Diversification is a key principle in managing an investment portfolio and in reducing risk. Equal weighting is simple yet powerful due to its transparency, beating mathematical optimization in a black box manner. Equal weight portfolios are constructed by subsetting top-N assets selected based on the rank of the return forecasts from deep neural networks, where the members are equally weighed.

The experiment shows that the deep ensemble model provides a way of portfolio selection with order of realized returns, outperforming the historical mean model.
Reference
  • DeMiguel, Victor, Lorenzo Garlappi, and Raman Uppal. "Optimal versus naive diversification: How inefficient is the 1/N portfolio strategy?" The review of Financial studies 22(5) 1915-1953 (2009).
  • Sang Il Lee, Deeply equall-weighted subset portfolios, https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.14402 (2020).
